The European Innovation Council has selected EQT to manage a new €5 billion fund for European deep-tech scaleups.

The Scaleup Europe Fund will provide growth capital for high-potential companies in strategic sectors, including artificial intelligence, quantum computing, and clean energy. It aims to bridge the late-stage financing gap that often forces European startups to seek investment outside the continent.

For those in the public sector and AI industries, this matters because it supports the development of a self-sufficient European tech ecosystem. It provides the necessary capital to move deep-tech innovations from the research phase to large-scale industrial application within Europe.
