The EU is focusing on reducing its reliance on technology from outside the bloc.

The Joint Research Centre (JRC) recently summarized how scientific research helped shape the Technological Sovereignty Package. The report looks at the risks of depending on non-EU semiconductor suppliers and the economic impact of recent chip shortages.

For those in the public sector, this highlights the need for stronger domestic supply chains and open-source solutions. It shows that building digital resilience is a key part of securing critical infrastructure like cloud services and AI.

How is your organization approaching the goal of technological sovereignty?
