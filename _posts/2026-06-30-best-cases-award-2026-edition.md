The Public Sector Tech Watch has announced the 2026 Best Cases Award for European public administrations.

This award recognizes projects that use emerging technologies like AI, Blockchain, and Digital Twins to improve public services. It looks for solutions that enhance citizen interactions, internal government processes, or policy making across Europe.

For AI practitioners and public sector leaders, this initiative creates a space to share knowledge and gain visibility. It highlights practical examples of how new tech can solve real problems, helping other organizations understand what works.
