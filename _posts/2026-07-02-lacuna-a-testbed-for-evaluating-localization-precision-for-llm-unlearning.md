Researchers have developed a new way to test if AI models truly forget sensitive information.

The study introduces LACUNA, a testbed that checks if unlearning methods actually remove data from a model's internal weights. Most current methods only confirm that a model stops outputting certain facts, which may just hide the data rather than erasing it.

This distinction is important for public sector privacy and security. If a model only hides data rather than erasing it, it remains vulnerable to attacks that can bring that information back. LACUNA provides a method to verify true data removal to meet privacy standards.

How can we ensure public sector AI meets strict data deletion requirements?
