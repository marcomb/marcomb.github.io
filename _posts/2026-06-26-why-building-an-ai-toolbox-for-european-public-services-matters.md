The European Commission is launching an AI Toolbox to help public administrations move beyond just testing new technology.

The toolbox provides a shared repository of open-source tools, use cases, and guidance on procurement and governance. It helps public services across Europe find reusable resources to make AI systems work together more effectively.

Link: https://interoperable-europe.ec.europa.eu/collection/ai-public-sector/ai-toolbox

Here is why it matters: the focus is moving from the technology itself to institutional readiness. Successful adoption depends on fixing data fragmentation, building internal skills, and establishing clear accountability. A solid data and governance foundation is the necessary first step for trustworthy AI.
