New reports and consensus statements are providing more structure for AI risk management.

Recent updates include detailed threat maps for loss-of-control scenarios and a new consensus statement on AI evaluation practices. These resources identify specific risks like monitoring subversion and training sabotage while establishing 27 standard practices for building and reporting AI evaluations.

For the public sector, this shift toward concrete frameworks is important. Standardized evaluations and structured threat maps provide a clearer basis for governance. These tools help move the conversation toward evidence-based oversight and measurable safety standards.
