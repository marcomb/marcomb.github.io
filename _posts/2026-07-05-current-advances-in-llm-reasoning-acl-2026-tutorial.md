Researchers are developing new ways to measure and improve how Large Language Models (LLMs) reason through complex tasks.

For those working in the public sector, these advancements are important for safety and reliability. Techniques that allow a model to refuse to answer when it is uncertain are key for high-stakes applications where factual accuracy is non-negotiable.
