Here is a look at how AI usage patterns align with human daily rhythms.

Anthropic's new Economic Index report breaks down how people interact with AI throughout the day. The data shows that usage follows a clear workweek pattern, with business tasks like email drafting peaking during office hours and personal requests like recipes or sleep advice appearing in the evenings. It also categorizes the types of outputs people create, ranging from technical code to long-form reports and explanations.

For those in the public sector or AI strategy, this data is useful for understanding how AI is being integrated into real-world workflows. It shows a shift toward more complex, "agentic" tasks rather than just simple chat. Understanding these usage cadences helps in planning public service automation and identifying where AI can best support high-value work.
