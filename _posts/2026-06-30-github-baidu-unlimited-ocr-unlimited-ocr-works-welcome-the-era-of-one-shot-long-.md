Baidu released Unlimited-OCR to handle long-horizon document parsing.

The tool handles complex tasks like multi-page PDF parsing and large-scale image extraction. It supports batch processing and different configurations for single images or entire documents.

This matters for the public sector because it simplifies the digitization of large archives and complex administrative forms. It helps maintain data consistency by keeping the context of the entire document together.

Could this simplify how you handle large-scale document digitisation?
