The European Commission uses the Technical Support Instrument to provide technical expertise to Member States facing complex reform challenges.

The program focuses on modernizing public administrations and creating a better environment for small businesses. It targets specific goals like closing skill gaps and managing the transition toward a digital economy across all 27 Member States.

For those in the public sector, this is important because it shows where resources are being directed to build internal capacity. These initiatives aim to help governments develop the skills needed to manage large-scale technological changes effectively.

It provides a clear roadmap for modernizing public service delivery and infrastructure across the EU.
