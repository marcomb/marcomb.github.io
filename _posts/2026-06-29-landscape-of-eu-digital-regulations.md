The EU has established a comprehensive legal framework to govern data, privacy, and digital services.

This landscape includes key regulations like the GDPR for personal privacy, the Data Governance Act for trusted data sharing, and the Data Act for connected devices. These rules aim to make digital transformation safer and more transparent across all Member States.

For public sector leaders, this matters because it provides a clear roadmap for using public data. It creates the trust needed to share information for the common good, such as in healthcare or infrastructure, while ensuring everyone follows harmonised rules.
