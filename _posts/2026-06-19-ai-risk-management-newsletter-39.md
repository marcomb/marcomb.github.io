Recent developments show a clear focus on establishing safety standards and risk management for frontier AI.

The latest updates include Google DeepMind's new security frameworks for AI agents and a new US project to create standards for cyber and biological risks. Additionally, the UK government is developing five different scenarios for 2030 to help policymakers stress-test future plans.

These updates matter because they move the conversation from abstract risk to practical infrastructure. For public sector leaders, these standards and stress-testing models provide a clearer path for creating reliable and safe AI governance.
